About us

Welcome to Psistaries Kapatsos, the premier destination for an extraordinary culinary experience in Limassol, Cyprus. With three conveniently located shops, we bring you a fusion of Cypriot, Greek, and International cuisine like no other.

At Kapatsos, we pride ourselves on our vast array of delectable dishes, offering an experience unlike any other. We are pioneers in introducing the concept of a rotisserie restaurant to Cyprus, where you can savor an extensive selection of mouthwatering creations. Our commitment to variety is unparalleled, as we boast the largest food buffet among all rotisserie restaurants in the country. Indulge in our remarkable buffet, where every dish tells a story and invites you to savor the flavors of Cyprus, Greece, and beyond.

Our Menu

Our restaurant takes pride in offering the largest rotisserie buffet in Cyprus, providing a wide selection of Cypriot, Greek, and international dishes. The restaurant offers a variety of options like meat, seafood, pulses, salads, and starters, ensuring that there is something for everyone regardless of their dietary preferences.

While we strive to offer our full menu at all locations, some dishes are seasonal or may only be available at select shops. Please inquire with our staff for more information.

Looking for catering services?

At Kapatsos, we understand that sometimes you just want to enjoy a delicious meal in the comfort of your own home, without the hassle of cooking. That's why we offer almost all of the dishes on our menu for catering. Whether you're looking to enjoy a quiet evening or have a larger event to cater, our food is perfect for any occasion.

For those hosting larger groups, we offer a variety of catering options that can be tailored to your specific needs. Our catering options are perfect for birthday parties, family gatherings, weddings, team building events, office meetings, or even daily meal plans for employees. We can set up the food on serving platters or in individual portions, depending on your preference. Our team is always happy to work with you to create a custom menu that fits your preferences and budget.

When you choose to take advantage of our takeout and catering services, you can trust that you'll receive the same high-quality food and service that we offer in our restaurant. We use only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients in our dishes, ensuring that each bite is packed with flavor and made with love. So, whether you're craving a lamb dish or a refreshing salad, we have something for everyone to enjoy.

For questions, prices, and bookings, please email us at info@kapatsos.com or call +357 25 562256.
